About us

With experience of managing both small and large scale events across the globe, Tyler Events can breathe life and imagination into your ideas, creating a personal event management service to meet your requirements.

Our venue finding team understands the demands of the current market place and uses our increased buying power and constantly evolving supplier network to source the right venue, in the right location.

Our event management team has vast experience of working with a multitude of projects, from webinars and online meetings, through to large exhibitions and conferences. Utilizing the latest technology our team can assist you with planning, development and delivering. We are driven to provide a seamless delegate experience.

Our client portfolio includes customers from the legal, automotive, charity and financial sectors. As a business we strive to develop outstanding relationships with client and suppliers and are proud to hold the Investors in People accreditation as well as membership to the HBAA.
